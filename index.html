about me

I'm currently a part of the product team at DataFeedWatch, a feed marketing startup.

My fields of expertise range from quantitative and qualitative user research to interaction design and high-fidelity prototyping.

I believe that good design never happens alone, which is why I'm a huge fan of UX workshops, such as design studio.

As a master of psychology, I have a strong background in research methodologies and design psychology. My academic research at the Jagiellonian University covered human-computer interaction and UX.

After hours, I work on my coding skills and explore the world of speciality coffee. I'm a proud father of two fluffy marimos :)
